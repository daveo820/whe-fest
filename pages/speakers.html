Community Opening — Health Success Stories & Open Mic

A Welcoming, Participatory Opening

WHE Fest opens with a community-centered session where attendees share health success stories, doctors and practitioners present solutions, and community members introduce themselves. This is your festival — step up to the mic.

This session sets the tone for the entire festival: collaborative, participatory, and centered on the collective power of women and mothers leading change.
