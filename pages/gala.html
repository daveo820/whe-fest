The Evening

A Night to Remember

The WHE Fest Saturday Evening Gala is the crown jewel of the festival — a glamorous, meaningful celebration held in the Grand Ballroom of the Gaylord National Resort overlooking the Potomac River.

Gather with extraordinary women for an evening of dancing, cocktails, and an organic feast — surrounded by the power and beauty of women who are leading the health revolution in America.

The Gala is not just a party. It's a declaration. It's the moment we pause from the work of changing the world to celebrate the fact that we already are.
