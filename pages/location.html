Getting Here

Transportation & Parking

✈

By Air

Three major airports serve the DC metro area:

  • DCA — Reagan National (15 min via metro)
  • IAD — Dulles International (45 min by car)
  • BWI — Baltimore-Washington (35 min by car or MARC train)
🚇

By Metro

The DC Metro is the easiest way to navigate the city.

  • Mt Vernon Sq / 7th St — Green & Yellow Line
  • 2-minute walk to the Convention Center
  • Service from all major airports
  • Day passes available at all stations
🚗

By Car & Parking

Multiple parking options within walking distance:

  • Convention Center Parking Garage (on-site)
  • SpotHero & ParkWhiz app discounts
  • WHE Fest shuttle from partner hotels
  • Rideshare drop-off on 9th St NW
🚌

WHE Fest Shuttle

Complimentary shuttle service runs between all partner hotels and the convention center:

  • Every 20 minutes during event hours
  • Available to all ticket holders
  • Schedule provided with tickets
