The Venue

Where Elegance Meets the Potomac

The Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center is one of the most breathtaking event venues on the East Coast — a magnificent glass-enclosed resort rising above the Potomac River waterfront at National Harbor, just minutes from Washington, D.C.

With over 2,000 hotel rooms, a stunning 19-story glass atrium, world-class dining, a full-service spa, and more than 470,000 square feet of convention space, the Gaylord National offers every amenity for an extraordinary three-day experience.

  • 19-story glass atrium — spectacular interior
  • 470,000+ sq ft of event & convention space
  • 2,000+ hotel rooms on-site
  • Full-service spa & fitness center
  • Multiple waterfront dining venues
  • Potomac River views throughout

Getting Here

Transportation & Directions

By Air

Three major airports serve the Washington area:

  • DCA — Reagan National (20 min by car)
  • IAD — Dulles International (45 min by car)
  • BWI — Baltimore-Washington (30 min by car)

By Car

The Gaylord National is easily accessible by road:

  • From I-95/I-495: Exit 2A to MD-210 South
  • Valet & self-parking available on-site
  • National Harbor exit clearly marked
  • GPS: 201 Waterfront St, National Harbor, MD

By Water Taxi

The scenic route — from the DC Waterfront:

  • Potomac Riverboat service from Old Town Alexandria
  • Georgetown-to-National Harbor ferry (seasonal)
  • Drops directly at the resort's waterfront dock
  • A truly unforgettable arrival experience

Rideshare & Taxi

Easy rideshare access to the resort:

  • Uber & Lyft available throughout DC area
  • 20–25 min from downtown Washington, D.C.
  • Drop-off at main hotel entrance
  • Rideshare staging area clearly marked
