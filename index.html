About the Festival

A Historic
Gathering.
A Turning Point.

You are invited to a historic gathering of women shaping the future of America. WHE Fest is a festival-style health and activism conference led by pioneering female experts — a community-led movement of women, mothers, and health advocates reclaiming leadership in family and community health.

This three-day event brings together six leading health advocacy organizations — Moms Across America, Children's Health Defense, Stand for Health Freedom, Global Wellness Forum, Weston A. Price Foundation, and Environmental Health Trust — working together to empower communities through education, advocacy, and collaboration.

From a Lobby Day on Capitol Hill to expert panels on nutrition, environmental health, and medical freedom — to hands-on advocacy workshops and a Saturday evening fundraising dinner — WHE Fest is where women come together to build healthy communities and a healthy America.
