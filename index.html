About the Festival

A Historic
Gathering.
A Turning Point.

You are invited to a historic gathering of inspiring and powerful women shaping the future of America. Held strategically in Washington, D.C., six weeks before the midterm elections, WHE Fest is a festival-style health and activism conference led by pioneering female experts.

This three-day event is designed to empower mothers and women to reclaim the health of their families, communities, and nation. In collaboration with leading health advocacy organizations, WHE Fest brings together experts offering the best of their knowledge in holistic wellness, civic engagement, and community leadership.

Join us in learning holistic self-care, family health strategies, and community empowerment — all while celebrating together. Enjoy yoga sessions, curated workshops, cocktails, and a Saturday evening Gala featuring dancing and organic cuisine. Women are leading the way in building healthy communities and a healthy America. Join us.
