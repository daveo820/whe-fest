About the Festival

A Movement.
A Gathering.
A Turning Point.

WHE Fest is the premier Women Health Empowerment Festival — a national gathering of women who are done waiting for permission and ready to lead. Held in the heart of Washington, DC, this multi-day event unites health advocates, policy leaders, community changemakers, and everyday women who believe their intuition is a superpower.

Connected with Moms Across America and aligned with the MAHA movement, WHE Fest is where wellness meets sovereignty — and where women come to be seen, strengthened, and sent back into the world changed.
