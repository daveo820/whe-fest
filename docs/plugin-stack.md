# WHE Fest — Recommended WordPress Plugin Stack

## Theme Recommendation

**Kadence Theme** (Free + Pro)
- Fastest-loading WP theme family
- Full-site editing with block builder
- Easy for nonprofit staff to maintain
- Excellent Gutenberg compatibility
- ~$89/year for Pro (one-time or annual)

**Alternative:** GeneratePress Premium (~$59/year)
Both are lightweight, fast-loading, and require minimal dev to maintain.

## Core Plugin Stack

### Page Building
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Kadence Blocks** | Free / $79 per year Pro | Advanced block editor, custom layouts, animations |
| **Spectra** (by Brainstorm Force) | Free | Additional blocks (timeline, tabs, team) |

### Email Capture & CRM
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **FluentForms** | Free / $59/year | Form builder, email capture, sponsor inquiry form |
| **FluentCRM** | $129/year | Email list management, broadcast emails, automations |
| **Mailchimp for WP** | Free | Alternative: connects forms to Mailchimp lists |

> **Recommendation:** FluentForms + FluentCRM for self-hosted control, or FluentForms + Mailchimp for simplicity. Both integrate with newsletters and nonprofit email workflows.

### Ticketing / Event Registration
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **The Events Calendar** | Free + Pro ($99/year) | Event pages, calendar view, schedule display |
| **Ticket Tailor** (External SaaS) | ~2.5% + $0.65/ticket | Preferred ticketing platform — clean, nonprofit-friendly pricing, embeds on site |
| **Eventbrite** embed | Free | Most recognizable ticketing brand; higher fees |
| **GiveWP** | Free + $149/year | Donation collection, nonprofit receipts |

> **Recommendation:** Use **Ticket Tailor** as primary ticketing (best nonprofit pricing, full embed on site). Use **The Events Calendar Pro** for schedule display.

### SEO
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Rank Math SEO** | Free | Full SEO suite: meta tags, sitemaps, schema markup |
| **Redirection** | Free | URL redirects management |

### Performance
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **WP Rocket** | $59/year | #1 caching plugin, lazy load, minification |
| **Smush** or **ShortPixel** | Free / ~$9/month | Image compression & WebP conversion |
| **Cloudflare** | Free tier available | CDN, security, fast global delivery |

### Security
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Wordfence** | Free / $99/year | Malware scanning, firewall, login protection |
| **WP Activity Log** | Free | Track all backend activity (important for nonprofit accountability) |
| **UpdraftPlus** | Free / $70/year | Automated backups to Google Drive or Dropbox |

### Social & Media
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Social Snap** or **Monarch** | ~$29/year | Social share buttons on posts |
| **Smash Balloon** | $49/year | Instagram/Facebook feed embeds |

### Utilities
| Plugin | Price | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Classic Widgets** or keep Gutenberg | Free | Sidebar/footer widget areas |
| **WP Consent API** | Free | Cookie consent compliance (GDPR/CCPA) |
| **PDF Embedder** | Free | Embed sponsorship packet PDF directly on page |
| **TablePress** | Free | Clean data tables (schedule, ticket comparisons) |

## Hosting Recommendation

| Provider | Plan | Price | Why |
|----------|------|-------|-----|
| **SiteGround** Growth | Managed WP | ~$30/month | Excellent uptime, free SSL, easy WP install |
| **WP Engine** Startup | Managed WP | ~$30/month | Premium performance, staging environment |
| **Flywheel** Freelance | Managed WP | ~$30/month | Clean dashboard, easy client handoff |

> For a nonprofit launching a major festival, **WP Engine** or **Flywheel** is recommended — managed hosting means no manual updates and guaranteed uptime during ticket launches.

## What This Stack Gives You

- **Fast site** — sub-2-second load times
- **Easy editing** — any team member can update speakers, schedule, copy
- **Scalable** — can handle traffic spikes during ticket launches
- **Secure** — automated backups + firewall
- **Low maintenance** — managed hosting handles server, updates handled by plugins
- **SEO-ready** — out of the box rankings foundation
